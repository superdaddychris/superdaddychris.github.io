ARTIST BIO

SuperDaddyChris is an independent hip hop artist, cinematic storyteller, and visual creator known for blending emotional music with film-inspired worlds. Through deeply personal songwriting and immersive visual storytelling, SuperDaddyChris creates music centered around love, heartbreak, regret, redemption, and self-discovery. His work combines emotional realism with cinematic worldbuilding, transforming songs into interconnected experiences that feel more like chapters of a movie than traditional music releases. His 2026 project, Love Me Back, is a cinematic album and visual series exploring the emotional collapse of a relationship through interconnected songs, evolving color themes, and narrative-driven music videos. Every aspect of the project—from lyrics and story concepts to visual direction and creative strategy—is personally developed by SuperDaddyChris.
