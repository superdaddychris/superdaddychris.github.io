SUPERDADDYCHRIS

Creator. Videographer. Artist. Entrepreneur.

SuperDaddyChris is a recording artist, content creator, videographer, editor, entrepreneur, U.S. Army servicemember, and founder whose work spans music, media, entertainment, and technology. Born in Chicago and later rooted in Houston, Texas, he built his career by documenting stories, producing videos, collaborating with creators and artists, and creating projects designed to connect audiences through storytelling.

The Creator Era

With a camera and a vision, SuperDaddyChris began documenting the people, places, and personalities around him. What started as a hobby evolved into years of content creation, interviews, travel videos, behind-the-scenes productions, and creator collaborations.

Videography & Collaborations

He has worked with artists, entertainers, entrepreneurs, and creators, producing and editing visual content across music, lifestyle, dance, and branded storytelling. Among those collaborations, he shot and edited several dance videos for Osmara Rojas, whose background includes time as a Houston Texans Cheerleader and a Miami Heat dancer before moving into acting in Los Angeles.

Artist & Music Vision

As an artist, SuperDaddyChris creates music driven by emotion, storytelling, and cinematic world-building. His work blends personal reflection with visual ambition, turning songs into experiences. His album Love Me Back expands his identity beyond music alone, showing him as both performer and visual architect.

LOVE ME BACK

  • 13-track cinematic album experience
  • Themes: heartbreak, regret, growth, accountability, acceptance
  • Visual worlds: Velvet Noir, Hellwave, Cry Wolf, Are We Through, Perfect Girl

Gaming & Pop Culture

SuperDaddyChris is also connected to creative projects in gaming and pop culture, including My Morphin Life Video Game, a project tied to the world of Jason David Frank. This reflects his ability to move across entertainment spaces and bring his presence into media beyond music videos and social content.

Military Service

In addition to his creative work, SuperDaddyChris serves in the United States Army. His discipline, resilience, and commitment to growth have shaped the way he approaches both life and art.

Founder Mindset

Beyond the camera and microphone, he is a builder focused on long-term vision. His work as an entrepreneur and founder reflects a drive to create platforms, brands, and opportunities that reach beyond entertainment into community, innovation, and legacy.
