SuperDaddyChris

Artist. Creator. Videographer. Editor. Entrepreneur. Army Servicemember. Founder.

Biography

SuperDaddyChris is a recording artist, content creator, videographer, editor, entrepreneur, United States Army servicemember, and founder whose work spans music, media, entertainment, storytelling, and technology.

Born in Chicago and later building roots in Houston, Texas, SuperDaddyChris developed his creative vision through years of filming, editing, documenting, producing, and creating. Before stepping fully into music, he built a foundation behind the camera, capturing artists, dancers, creators, entrepreneurs, and entertainers.

His work includes music videos, creator collaborations, interviews, behind-the-scenes content, dance videos, visual storytelling, and digital media projects. He has also shot and edited several dance videos for Osmara Rojas, a former Houston Texans Cheerleader and Miami Heat Dancer who later moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting.

As an artist, SuperDaddyChris creates emotional, cinematic music built around heartbreak, growth, love, regret, accountability, and transformation. His project Love Me Back represents a major creative chapter, blending music, film-style visuals, storytelling, and personal emotion into one connected world.

SuperDaddyChris is also connected to the world of entertainment and gaming through My Morphin Life Video Game, a project connected to Jason David Frank, expanding his presence beyond music and video into digital entertainment.

Beyond entertainment, he is building the ME app, a social platform concept focused on identity, creators, profiles, rooms, following tools, dating features, content, and user connection.

His journey represents more than one title. SuperDaddyChris is a creator building across multiple lanes: music, media, video, technology, entrepreneurship, military service, and personal legacy.
