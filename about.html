SUPERDADDYCHRIS

Artist • Storyteller • Visual Creator

SuperDaddyChris is an independent artist, storyteller, and visual creator blending emotional music with cinematic AI-driven visuals.

Known for combining raw lyricism with dark cinematic aesthetics, his work explores themes of love, heartbreak, obsession, regret, and personal growth.

His project "Love Me Back" combines music, film-inspired storytelling, and immersive visual worlds into one emotional experience.
