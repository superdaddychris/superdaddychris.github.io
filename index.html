BIO

SuperDaddyChris is an independent hip hop artist, cinematic storyteller, Army servicemember, videographer, editor, and creator from Chicago by way of Houston.

His music blends emotional storytelling, heartbreak, accountability, and cinematic visuals, led by the Love Me Back project.

Notable Work

His creative background includes music videos, Houston creator interviews, dance visuals, and appearances connected to My Morphin Life.
